Zero tolerance for intoxicating substances


At our Uber Black transportation service, the safety of our customers and drivers is our top priority. For this reason, we maintain a strict zero-tolerance policy for the use of intoxicating substances.

It is strictly prohibited for any driver to operate a vehicle under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or any other substance that may compromise their ability to drive safely. If a customer suspects that a driver is under the influence of intoxicating substances, they are advised to terminate the trip immediately and report the incident to Uber and the appropriate authorities.

Reports of violations of this policy are taken extremely seriously and may result in suspension or removal of the driver's access to the platform. We are committed to providing a safe, comfortable, and professional environment for everyone.

Filing Complaints with the City of Dallas


If you would like to file a complaint or report a problem with the City of Dallas, you can easily do so by calling 3-1-1. This service is available for residents and visitors to report concerns related to transportation, street maintenance, and other city issues.

Steps to file your complaint:

  • Call 3-1-1: Dial the number from any phone and follow the operator's instructions.
  • Provide details: Make sure you have all relevant information about your complaint on hand, such as the exact location, the nature of the problem, and any other details that might help resolve it.
  • Receive a tracking number: When you file your complaint, you will be provided with a tracking number that you can use to check the status of your report in the future.
  • Important: This service is part of Ordinance 29696 and is designed to improve the quality of life in our community. We appreciate your help in making Dallas a safer and more enjoyable place for everyone.


If you have any questions about how to use this service, please feel free to contact us. At our company, we are committed to providing you with exceptional service and ensuring your satisfaction on every trip.
